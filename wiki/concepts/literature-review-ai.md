# AI-Assisted Literature Review

AI-assisted literature review uses LLMs and specialized tools to accelerate the process of finding, reading, summarizing, and synthesizing academic literature.

## Context & Background

Literature review is one of the most time-consuming aspects of academic research. AI tools can assist at every stage:

- **Search**: AI-powered academic search engines (Semantic Scholar, Elicit, Consensus)
- **Summarization**: Condensing papers into key findings and methods
- **Synthesis**: Identifying themes and connections across multiple papers
- **Gap identification**: Finding what hasn't been studied
- **Citation management**: Organizing and formatting references

## Practical Implications

- **Verify all citations**: AI frequently fabricates paper titles, authors, and findings — verify everything
- **Use AI for synthesis, not search alone**: The real value is in connecting ideas across papers
- **Combine tools**: Use Semantic Scholar for search, NotebookLM for deep reading, LLMs for synthesis
- **Read key papers yourself**: AI summaries are no substitute for deep reading of foundational papers in your area
