# Future of Academic Publishing

The future of academic publishing examines how AI tools are transforming the creation, review, and dissemination of academic knowledge — from AI-assisted writing to automated peer review.

## Context & Background

AI is affecting every stage of academic publishing:

- **Writing**: AI-assisted drafting accelerates manuscript preparation
- **Peer review**: AI can simulate reviewer feedback and assist editors
- **Replication**: AI makes it easier to replicate and verify published results
- **Dissemination**: AI-generated summaries and translations broaden audience reach
- **Volume**: The potential for AI to dramatically increase submission volumes raises quality concerns

## Practical Implications

- **Prepare for changing norms**: Journal policies on AI use are evolving rapidly
- **Develop AI review skills**: Reviewers will need to evaluate AI-assisted manuscripts
- **Invest in replicability**: AI makes it easier for others to check your work — ensure it holds up
- **Consider AI-first formats**: Structured, machine-readable papers may become preferred
